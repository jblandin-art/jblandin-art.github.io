Hobby Page

What

Images are amazing. I enjoy capturing moments and telling stories through pictures and video. Over the years, I have shot hundreds of music videos and sports events, and I’ve edited many other types of videos, including AMVs and game montages.

Who

I'm Josiah Blanding. Growing up playing video games shaped my creativity and later influenced how I learned video production and editing. Gaming helped me discover my passion for storytelling through visuals.

Where

I have shot projects all across North Carolina – from Catawba College to Forest Park, throughout every corner of Charlotte, and many other locations. My freelance work has taken me everywhere in the state.

How

I began learning After Effects and other editing software as a kid while playing video games. Competitive gaming and montages inspired me to create my own videos, and these experiences remain some of my fondest memories.

When

Most of my freelance work occurred from 2018 to 2023. While I have stepped back from shooting professionally, I continue to enjoy editing and capturing photos for fun.

Why

I often experience strong emotions, and creating is my outlet. It brings me closer to my inner child, allowing me to feel peace and satisfaction through the process of visual storytelling.
