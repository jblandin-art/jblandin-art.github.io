Client Project Proposal

Project Overview

This website is being developed for an auto detailing business owned and operated by my brother. The purpose of the website is to introduce potential customers to the company, showcasing the quality of services and helping establish professional credibility through a well-designed online presence. The website will allow users to browse available detailing packages and pricing, view examples of previous work, and easily get in touch for inquiries or bookings.

The intended users of the website are vehicle owners seeking professional detailing services. Whether they need basic cleaning, extensive restoration, or specialty treatments, this platform will serve as a convenient resource for learning about the business, exploring options, and contacting the owner directly.

Site Map

Page Design

Home

Purpose: Introduce the business and point users to the services page.

Audience/Users: People looking for auto detailing.

Content: Hero image, slogan, brief intro, one button linking to Services.

Data Entry: None.

Buttons/Links: One button to Services.

Actions: Button click goes to Services page.


About

Purpose: Show who runs the business and why customers can trust them.

Audience/Users: Potential customers.

Content: Short text about the owner, mission, maybe a picture or two.

Data Entry: None.

Buttons/Links: Links to Services or Contact.

Actions: Clicking links navigates to those pages.


Services

Purpose: Show what services are offered and their prices.

Audience/Users: Customers deciding what service to choose.

Content: List of services with short descriptions, prices, small images or icons.

Data Entry: None.

Buttons/Links: Optional “Book Now” links or scroll to Contact info.

Actions: Clicking buttons scrolls or navigates to Contact.


Gallery

Purpose: Show examples of work to demonstrate quality.

Audience/Users: Customers evaluating service.

Content: Before-and-after images in a simple interactive gallery.

Data Entry: None.

Buttons/Links: Clicking a photo enlarges it.

Actions: JS enlarges images or opens a lightbox.


Contact

Purpose: Let customers see how to reach the business.

Audience/Users: People who want to call or email.

Content: Phone number, email, location, hours.

Data Entry: None.

Buttons/Links: Clickable phone and email links.

Actions: Clicking phone opens call app; clicking email opens mail client.

Dynamic Functionality

On the Gallery page, JavaScript will let users hover over images to show small captions or additional details. On the Services and Gallery pages, small interactive feedback will appear when hovering over images or buttons, such as highlighting a “Most popular!” service. Additionally, fade-in effects will be used on scroll so content like service descriptions and gallery images gradually appear as the user moves down the page. These features are simple to implement and make the site feel more dynamic and engaging.
